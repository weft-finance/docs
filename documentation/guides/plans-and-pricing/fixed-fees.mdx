## **Fixed Fees**

Fixed fees (also called Service Fees) are charges with a set price that are billed on a defined schedule or event, regardless of the amount of usage a customer has accumulated. In Vayu, fixed fees can be billed using four payment models:Â 

- One-Time Fee
- Subscription
- Contract Terms
- Commission

A One-Time Fee is a fixed, single charge, typically used for things like setup, onboarding, implementation, or an upfront service charge (a contract can include multiple one-time fees).

#### **_Example_**

In cases where the subscription is calendar-aligned (so the billing cycle resets on a specific date each month), consider a scenario when a customer subscribes monthly, beginning on July 14:

- The first invoice to be generated covers the period between July 14 to July 31 (the final date of the month)
- The next invoice will cover August 1 through August 31 (the full calendar month)
- All future invoices will be generated for full calendar months.
