---
title: "Xero"
---

Vayu supports a full integration with Xero, including: exporting approved invoices, allowing usage-based and fixed charges to appear as structured invoice line items. Customers and products map to their Xero equivalents, enabling teams to complete billing and accounting workflows directly in Xero.
